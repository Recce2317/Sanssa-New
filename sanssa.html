Go Teach Training Course

Duration : 8x90min

Description :

The SANSSA Teacher Training Course is a skills training course for Sunday School teachers. This course has been designed to provide the specialist training required for teachers of pupils in the various age categories. Upon enrolment teachers select the department group of their choice and meet with that group for the duration of the course. The course is designed to meet the needs of both experienced teachers and those who have just started Sunday School teaching. However, experience has shown that teachers benefit most from the course when they have had previous exposure to Sunday School teaching, even for a short period. A certificate, issued by the respective SANSSA Regional Office, is awarded to all trainees who attend at least seven out of the eight sessions.


Course Objectives

The overall objective of the course is to provide Sunday School teachers with the fundamental skills required to teach the Word of God to pupils at the particular age-level with whom they are working.


Sessions :

  • The Goal of the Sunday SchoolLearning or ListeningAge Group CharacteristicsAge Group Teaching MethodsAge Group Visual AidsAge Group Lesson PreparationThe Teacher as Personal Worker
  • Making Christ Personal

Language

English

Afrikaans


Age Groups

Pre-Primary (Ages 4-6 Years)

Primary (Grade 1-3)

Junior (Grade 4-6)

Senior (Grade 7-12)


Audience

Sunday School Teachers


Time Arrangements

The recommended time arrangement is one session per week for eight consecutive weeks.


Presentation Arrangements

The course is presented by a team of training leaders, appointed by the SANSSA Branch or Region. Manuals, available to leaders, provide both content and presentation suggestions and serve as guides to fulfilling the objectives of each session. Presentation is by means of a variety of teaching techniques with particular emphasis on those methods which will involve the trainees in meaningful learning activities. The demonstration of techniques and materials and the use of real–life illustrations also form an integral part of the course. Each trainee attending the course, receives a manual containing outline notes of each session.

Discover Leadership Course

Duration : 4x90min

Description :

The ever-increasing demand for teacher training requires that SANSSA Branches and Regions constantly discover and develop new training leaders. The Training Leaders’ Course has been designed to meet this need and should be viewed as PHASE ONE of an on-going programme of training for training leaders. PHASE TWO, a follow-up to this course, will provide more specific instruction in the content and procedures of the SANSSA Teacher Training Course. PHASE THREE would include any refresher course-type training which may be provided for registered SANSSA training leaders from time to time.


Course Objectives

The purpose of the course is to provide basic training for trainers of Sunday School teachers. During the course, participants will be given an insight into: the aim and content of a basic “how to” course for Sunday School teachers the role and responsibility of training leaders in planning and presenting training sessions. The course also provides the means whereby Branches may identify suitably-qualified training leaders whom they may invite to assist with the SANSSA training programme.
